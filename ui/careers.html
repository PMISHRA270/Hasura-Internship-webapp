Various Career Opportunities

Composer/Arranger


Composers create original music,and arrangers take existing music and decide which parts each instrumentalist will play.Many Composers are also arrangers.


Disc Jockey

Why just play tunes for yourself when you can play them for your audience while earning money.Not to mention that you get to entertain listeners with your effervescent personality and impress them with your musical knowledge.


Music Teacher


Music teacher instructs people of all ages on how to play and appreciate music.This position lets you pass on your passion of music to others.

Recording Engineer


A recording engineer operates and sets up the recording equipment used to capture and shape the sound of an album.

Mix Engineer


Mix engineers mix the various sound elements of a track to balance the volume and improve the audio quality.


Rockstar


A rockstar is a performer in the band who commands the most attention onstage,plays a key part in writing the band's songs,and serves as the group's spokesperson.


Lyricist


A Lyricist writes song's lyrics which are used in live shows,film/TV,and commercials.
